# Beta Feedback Questions

Use this form after users complete at least one full workflow.

## 1-5 Score Questions (1 = poor, 5 = excellent)
1. How easy was initial setup (login, connect, sync)?
2. How easy was tracking your first ASIN?
3. How useful was Keyword Rank Tracker for your daily work?
4. How useful was Product/BSR Refresh?
5. How useful was Buy Box Monitor?
6. How useful were Alerts?
7. How clear were the results shown in each module?
8. How confident are you in the data quality for decisions?
9. How fast did pages and actions feel overall?
10. Overall beta experience score.

## Open-Ended Questions
1. Which tool was most useful, and why?
2. What was confusing or unclear?
3. Which missing feature matters most to you right now?
4. What would make you pay for this product?
5. What one change would most improve your weekly usage?

## Severity Guidance
- P0: Core flow blocked, cannot continue.
- P1: Major function broken, workaround weak.
- P2: Usable but incorrect/confusing in places.
- P3: Cosmetic/polish issue.
